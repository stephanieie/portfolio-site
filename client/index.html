Stephanie Fereiro

Hello there! If you happen to stumble upon this site, please note that its layout, design, and content are a work in progress. I am builting this site from scratch while I learn to code. Thanks for stopping by!

Stephanie Fereiro is a freelance writer and editor based in Ontario. She graduated from Ryerson University with a Bachelor of Journalism and English Minor in 2012. This September, she will begin a post-graduate certificate program in Integrated Marketing Communications at Conestoga College. Stephanie designs and sells original embroidered pouches on Etsy and at Victoire Boutique locations in Toronto and Ottawa.

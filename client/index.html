I am a freelance writer, copy editor, and fact checker based in Ontario. I am currently enrolled in a post-graduate certificate program in Integrated Marketing Communications at Conestoga College.

I graduated from Ryerson University with a Bachelor of Journalism and English Minor in 2012.

I lived in London, England from May 2012 to June 2013.

In my spare time, I design and sell original embroidered pouches on Etsy and at Victoire Boutique locations in Toronto and Ottawa.

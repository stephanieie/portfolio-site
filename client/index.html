Stephanie Fereiro

Hello there! I'm Stephanie Fereiro, a communications professional based in Kitchener, Ontario. I recently completed the post-graduate Integrated Marketing Communications certificate program at Conestoga College. I'd love to hear from you, so please feel free to contact me.

I graduated from Ryerson University with a Bachelor of Journalism and English Minor in 2012.

I love to explore, and I lived in London, England for over a year.

I am truly passionate about communicating, and I have over a decade of experience in customer service.

In my spare time, I design and sew original embroidered pouches. You can find them on Etsy.
